Baofeng Mini 5 — Web Bluetooth proof-of-concept

Validates the full stack in your browser: Web Bluetooth → GATT → program mode → identify → read memory. A successful block read proves both halves of cloning (download) work with no cable, ESP32, or CHIRP.

  1. Use Chrome or Edge (Web Bluetooth — not Safari/Firefox).
  2. Radio on, Bluetooth enabled, not connected to the phone app.
  3. Click Connect & Read, then pick the radio in the chooser.

Idle.

Service 0xFFE0 / characteristic 0xFFE1 (HM-10 transparent serial). Write Without Response; ACK = 0x06.
